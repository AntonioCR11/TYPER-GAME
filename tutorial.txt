	 The Gameplay itself is simple
You just have to type the words that displayed
	      as FAST as you can

	 You can't just be fast but you
   have to be precise with your typing too
	since your score and typing speed
 will be calculated based on the right answer

  This is how your speed will be calculated
	   ============================
	   |(SCORE/((PLAY_TIME/60)*5))|
	   ============================
	 You can change the play time at
   the "SET TIME" menu on main menu screen
	You can also cutom the words in
	       the "LIBRARY" menu
